🔖The Details Tag ↓
The details tag is used to create a collapsible section. You can then include the title inside of a summary tag.
✈️Combined with Tailwind ↓
We can easily create a great looking collapsible section that also includes the slide down animation.
👨‍💻The Code Is Simple ↓
Creating this simple collapsible section is very simple and doesn't even require any javascript.
